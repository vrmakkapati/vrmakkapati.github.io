Lecture Series on Learning and Control

The lecture series will be run in a virtual format via Zoom. Each talk will be of around 45 minutes, followed by Q&A of 10-15 minutes. All talks will be recorded and uploaded on the webpage/youtube for later views.

Please register for the lecture series by filling out this Google form.

Talks (all times are in IST)

Week 1

  • Introduction to Optimal Control by Mangal Kothari [video]
    Calculus of variations, Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman Equation, LQR, Pontryagin's minimum principle, Numerical methods

  • Introduction to Machine Learning by Muni Sreenivas Pydi [video]
    Supervised learning, Linear classifiers, Regularization, Bias-variance tradeoff, Gradient-based optimization, Basics of neural networks

Week 2

  • Optimal Control under Uncertainties by Venkata Ramana Makkapati [video]
    Feedback control, Neighboring optimal control, Desensitized optimal control

  • Deep Learning by Ashwin Kalyan [video]
    Image processing, Convolutional neural networks, Recurrent neural networks, Representation learning

Week 3

  • Introduction to Differential Games by Venkata Ramana Makkapati [https:youtu.be/H6nwSnCHipM video]
    Examples, Nash equilibrium, Zero-sum games, Differential games, Pursuit-Evasion games

  • Adversarial Machine Learning by Muni Sreenivas Pydi: 12th December, 2020. 8 PM [https:youtu.be/dMF6GvrkC48 video]
    Adversarial attacks on deep learning, Evasion attacks, Poisoning attacks, Certified defenses, Game-theoretic view of adversarial robustness

Week 4

  • Advanced Topics (Pursuit-Evasion Games) by Mangal Kothari: 18th December, 2020. 8 PM
    Reachability based pursuit-evasion, High speed evaders, Missile guidance

  • Reinforcement Learning by Ashwin Kalyan: 19th December, 2020. 8 PM
    Value / Policy iteration, Q-learning, Deep Q-learning, Policy gradients, Actor-critic policies

Week 5

  • Geometric Optimal Control by Ravi Banavar: 25th December, 2020. 8 PM
    (Guest lecture)

  • Information Theoretic Approaches to Optimal Representation Learning by Ramakrishna Vedantam: 26th December, 2020. 8 PM
    (Guest Lecture)

Week 6

  • Constrained Geometric Optimal Control by Ravi Banavar: 1st January, 2021. 8 PM
    (Guest lecture)

  • Guidance Strategies in Three Player Aerial Engagements by Ashwini Ratnoo: 2nd January, 2021. 8 PM
    Target-Defender-Missile problem, Line-of-sight guidance, Cooperation in defending team, Launch envelopes, Pure and mixed strategy game solutions

Week 7

  • A Game of Two Cars - I by Debraj Chakraborty‬: 8th January, 2021. 8 PM
    Turn radius constraints, Reachable sets, Feedback saddle point strategies

  • A Game of Two Cars - II by Debraj Chakraborty‬: 9th January, 2021. 8 PM
    Turn radius constraints, Reachable sets, Feedback saddle point strategies
